The price for accommodation will be deducted from your salary for the following months. You can live in our apartments as long as you need (usually we provide housing contracts for up to 6 months).

Deposit:

Upon arrival you need to pay a deposit of 600€ or 900€ ( this is the payment for accommodation per month, and we take this deposit for things that can be broken in the accommodation and it’s for repairs such things. But when you will be moving out of the accommodation and everything will be alright, we’ll return you that deposit ).

Discount:

There is a possibility to get a discount on accommodation if we also employ one of your friends/relatives/acquaintances. Every placed candidate = voucher of €100,- in rent - with maximum 4 vouchers per month.

If you are not satisfied with our prices, you can find accommodation on your own. For example, we suggest checking out the options on Airbnb.
